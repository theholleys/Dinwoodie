In September 2021, Ryan Dinwoodie purchased the Steve Beavis MOT & Repair Centre. Dinwoodie Motors is now proud to provide excellent levels of service to Stroud and the surrounding areas.

Ryan himself is a master technician and prior to owning Dinwoodie Motors, spent 8 years getting to know Beavis' clients whilst building his own client base.

These days Dinwoodie Motors employs 3 additional trained mechanics to carry out the work you need on your vehicle. Dinwoodie Motors can provide a variety of services, from MOTs and servicing, to diagnostics and repairs.

We have the facilities to MOT class 4, 5 and 7 vehicles. For all enquiries, give us a call or drop us an email. Our details are in the Contact Us section below.

Dinwoodie Motors Ltd is situated just off the canal down Chestnut Lane. If you drop your vehicle off with us it's only a short 5-10 minute walk into Stroud town centre
